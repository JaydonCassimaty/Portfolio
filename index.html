About Me



Hi, my name is Jaydon Cassimaty. I'm a recent graduate of a Bachelor of MultiMedia Design at UQ. What does that mean? Well it means I learned a variety of skills in both development and design which spread across most platforms! Over the course of my degree and personal projects I have dabbled in App design/development, Web Design/development, Graphic Design, Game Design/Development, UX/UI Design and last but not least Interaction design.

My passion lies in creating Architecturally integrated interactive technology to advertise the location opposed to advertising the products within.

Projects

Sky rail

Sky rail was 1 of 2 proposed installation for a multi purpose tower development on Cavil Avenue, the core entertainment precinct of the Gold Coast. The installation maps the movement of the light rail system to 53 floors of the tower, to provide quick and easy access to tram timetable information from the beach and the bars.
